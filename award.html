Awards and Recognition

Home Awards

During my education and research career, I have been recognized on a number occasions.

2018

Nominated to Represent The UK at "The 2018 Next Generation of Researchers in Power Systems" Symposium,
This symposium is to give young researchers in power systems an opportunity to meet in an informal setting, discuss new research ideas, and establish personal contacts that are essential to a successful scientific career. Attendance at this seminar is limited to about 20 young researchers from around the world and is by invitation only. The UK has two seats.

2017

Recipient of the British Council Travel Award
The award was provided by the British Council, based on a competitive selection process, and covered the costs including: travel, accommodation and subsistence related to the participation in the workshop on "Smart Grid Transformation of Existing Electricity Networks in the UK and Turkey: What are the Challenges and How They Change from Smart Cities to Smart Villages", 5-7 September 2017, Istanbul, Turkey.
