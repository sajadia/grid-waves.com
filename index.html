This is me

Amirhossein Sajadi

I am a junior faculty in System and Control Engineering at the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Department of Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, OH. I also hold a secondary appointment as a Hononary Fellow at the Power Systems Engineering Research Center (PSERC) of University of Wisconsin-Madison, WI. I completed the Ph.D. degree in Systems and Control Engineering at the Case Western Reserve University in May 2016 and the Postdoctoral Research at the University of Manchester (former UMIST) in August 2018.
