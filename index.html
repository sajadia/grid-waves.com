This is me

Amirhossein Sajadi

I am a junior faculty in Systems and Control Engineering at the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) Department of Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, OH.

I also hold a secondary appointment as a Hononary Fellow at the Power Systems Engineering Research Center (PSERC) of University of Wisconsin-Madison, WI.

My research interests evolves around the operation, stability, and control of dynamical systems, with the particular current emphasis on applications in electric power systems.

For Fall 2018 Semester, I will be teaching two courses:
EECS 374/474: Advanced Control and Energy Systems
EECS 375/475: Applied Control
